Welcome to Career Atlas

The project aims to develop an AI-based chatbot for career guidance and course recommendation. The chatbot utilizes natural language processing and machine learning algorithms to interact with users, understand their career aspirations, and suggest suitable courses and career paths. The system is designed to provide personalized recommendations based on the user's educational background, skills, and interests.
